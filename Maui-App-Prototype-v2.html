Korean BBQ Beef Bowl
★★★★★
Caramelised marinated beef over jasmine rice with pickled vegetables and gochujang drizzle. Bold, satisfying, built for batch prep.
Korean Lunch · Dinner
Prep
15 min
Cook
30 min
Serves
4
🔖
Save
😸
Rate
🔄
Swap
Ingredients
Instructions
Nutrition
Beef (thinly sliced)500g
Jasmine rice320g
Soy sauce3 tbsp
Sesame oil1 tbsp
Brown sugar2 tbsp
Garlic cloves4
Gochujang paste2 tbsp
Cucumber1
Spring onions3
1
Combine beef with soy sauce, sesame oil, brown sugar, garlic, and ginger. Marinate for at least 20 minutes.
2
Cook jasmine rice according to package instructions. Fluff with a fork and keep warm.
3
Heat a large pan or wok over high heat. Cook beef in batches until caramelised, about 3–4 minutes per side.
4
Thinly slice cucumber. Mix with a pinch of salt and rice vinegar for a quick pickle.
5
Divide rice into containers. Top with beef, pickled cucumber, spring onions, and a drizzle of gochujang.
